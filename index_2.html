More Than Accommodation

Un Día en la Finca isn't a resort or hotel—it's an authentic countryside experience where you'll stay with a local family in their home. Located in Mata Caliche, near San José de los Llanos, you'll experience real Dominican rural life with all the comforts you need.

Historical Significance

You'll be staying in a region of profound historical importance. San José de los Llanos holds the unique distinction as the location where Vicente Celestino Duarte first declared Dominican independence from Haitian rule on February 26, 1844—one day before the famous declaration in Santo Domingo. This makes it the site of the very first Independence Day in Dominican Republic history.

Your Daily Experience

Morning

Wake up to fresh countryside air and enjoy traditional Dominican breakfast with freshly ground coffee. Pick fruit from the property's coconut, orange, and avocado trees.

Afternoon

Relax by the swimming pool, explore the large deck area, or take your included rental car to nearby beaches and attractions. Enjoy authentic lunch prepared by your hosts.

Evening

Savor traditional dinner while sharing stories with your host family. Experience genuine Dominican hospitality in a peaceful countryside setting.

Explore the Region

Your rental car opens doors to beautiful beaches, historic cities, and natural wonders.

Boca Chica Beach

Famous for its turquoise, shallow waters and lively local atmosphere—perfect for swimming and enjoying fresh seafood.

Juan Dolio

A peaceful, family-friendly beach with local restaurants and a relaxed atmosphere.

San Pedro de Macorís

Historic city featuring the Malecón waterfront promenade, Central Park, cathedral, and unique cultural attractions.

Cueva de las Maravillas

Impressive cave system with fascinating geology and historical significance.
